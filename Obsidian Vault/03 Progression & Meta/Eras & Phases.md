# Eras & Phases

> **Purpose:** The discrete thematic stages of the climb. Each era reframes scale and debuts a major new system so the game stays fresh — not just "bigger numbers." Defines the complexity drip.
>
> **Depends on:** [[Progression Arc]]
> **Feeds into:** [[Milestones & Unlocks]], [[Narrative & Flavor]]

## Structure: Eras → Offices → Phases

- **4 Eras** (by scale) → **8 Offices** (2 per era) → **16 Elections** (each office = a **Primary** then a **General** phase, see [[Progression Arc]]).
- Every rung has both phases from the start; the *systems that make those races rich* layer in across eras (below).

## The Complexity Drip — one headline system per era

| Era | Offices | Headline Hook (what's NEW) | Flavor |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Local** | City Council, Mayor | **The core loop + Volunteers.** Knock-on-doors tap + crit; first Field (Canvasser, Phone Bank) & Finance (Small-Dollar Drive) generators; cash, voters, pool + timer + **1 rival**; **Volunteers** appear as a basic grassroots global multiplier (base accrual rate). Primary/general exist but *simplified*. | Retail politics — yard signs, handshakes, the purest underdog. |
| **County** | County Council, County Exec | **Coalitions & character.** **Interest groups & positioning** come online (court regional blocs; the radical-primary → moderate-general squeeze + flip-flop cost). **Charisma via minigames** debuts — personal-campaigning minigames that *both* shift interest-group support *and* build Charisma, which now **accelerates Volunteer gain** (paying off Local's setup). Rivals start rising. | Coalition-building; first "you can't please everyone." |
| **State** | State Legislature, Governor | **The machine.** Deployable **Abilities** (active tactical boosts) and **Events & Crises** (scandals, opportunities) begin; mid-tier generators (Regional Office, Campaign Bus, mid Finance rungs); the Charisma→Volunteer engine hits full swing. | Media attention; a real staffed operation under scrutiny. |
| **Federal** | Senate, President | **National stage.** **Ideological forks** (commit to an identity); grand generators (**National Media Team, Super PAC**); **max rivals** (multi-candidate cap); sharpest primary/general gap; full-intensity events; **Prestige** takes the spotlight (and seeds the post-MVP galactic reveal). | The national circus; absurd grandeur. |

**Prestige** accrues from *every* election win throughout the game, but becomes the headline meta-reward at the Federal tier and for replay. → [[Prestige & Reset System]].

## Reconciliation Notes

- **Volunteers (Local) before Charisma (County):** in the Local era, Volunteers accrue at a **fixed base rate** (Charisma effectively baseline). County introduces Charisma and the minigames that raise it, which then **scales** Volunteer accrual. Needs a defined base rate. → [[Primary Resources]], [[Number Scaling & Curves]].
- **Minigames live in County** (consequence of moving Charisma there): the same minigames serve as both the interest-group "answer" mechanic and the Charisma builder. → [[Primary Resources]], [[Policy, Platform & Interest Groups]].
