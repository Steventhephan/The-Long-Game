# 🏛️ The Long Game — Design Wiki

A mobile-first browser incremental game inspired by *Cookie Clicker*, themed around **politics, campaigns, institutions, and societal transformation**.

> This vault is a living game design document, built through a structured design interview. Each note below is filled out one at a time, in dependency order. Nothing here is final until its `status` reads `complete`.
>
> 🧭 **New session picking this up? Read [[Interview Process & Conventions]] first** — it holds the role, the interview rules, and the note conventions so you can continue seamlessly.

---

## 📚 Map of Content

### 01 — Foundation *(the "why" and "for whom")*
- [[Vision Statement]] — the one-line north star + elevator pitch
- [[Player Fantasy]] — what the player feels they are becoming
- [[Tone & Theme]] — satire vs. earnest, setting, political stance
- [[Target Audience & Platform]] — who plays, and the mobile-first constraints
- [[Design Pillars]] — 3–5 inviolable principles every decision is tested against

### 02 — Core Gameplay *(the moment-to-moment game)*
- [[Core Loop]] — the second-to-second and minute-to-minute loop
- [[Tap Mechanic]] — the "cookie" equivalent: what tapping does
- [[Primary Resources]] — currencies and what they represent
- [[Generators]] — automated producers (the "buildings")
- [[Upgrades]] — multipliers, unlocks, and modifiers

### 03 — Progression & Meta *(the long arc)*
- [[Progression Arc]] — the macro journey from start to endgame
- [[Eras & Phases]] — thematic stages of transformation
- [[Prestige & Reset System]] — the meta loop that gives the game its length
- [[Milestones & Unlocks]] — gating and the cadence of reveals
- [[Offline & Idle Progress]] — what happens while away

### 04 — Thematic Systems *(the political simulation layer)*
- [[Policy, Platform & Interest Groups]] — the central strategic system: positioning & coalitions
- [[Campaigns & Abilities]] — deployable active tools in the live race
- [[Minigames]] — debates/town halls/galas that build Charisma & shift support
- [[Events & Crises]] — dynamic interruptions and choices
- [[Opposition & Conflict]] — the rival-candidate AI

> *Cut from MVP scope: Institutions & Societal Transformation — the game is about winning offices, not governing.*

### 05 — Economy & Balance *(the math)*
- [[Economy Model]] — sources, sinks, and how currencies interrelate
- [[Number Scaling & Curves]] — cost growth and production scaling
- [[Pacing & Difficulty]] — the tuning of time-to-reward

### 06 — Experience Layer *(how it looks and feels)*
- [[UX & UI Layout]] — mobile screen layout and navigation
- [[Visual & Audio Style]] — art direction and sound
- [[Game Feel & Juice]] — feedback, animation, satisfaction
- [[Narrative & Flavor]] — flavor text, voice, messaging

### 07 — Production *(written for the implementing AI)*
- 🛠️ **[[Build Quickstart]] — START HERE TO BUILD** (stack, repo, data model as TypeScript, constants, tick algorithm, save schema, Phase-1 DoD)
- [[Project Summary]] — self-contained synthesis of the whole design
- [[Technical Architecture Requirements]] — stack, data model, save format
- [[Implementation Roadmap]] — build order and milestones

> 🧭 **New session here to *build* (not design)?** The design is complete and decision-complete — open **[[Build Quickstart]]** and start coding the Phase-1 vertical slice. Do not re-run the interview.
